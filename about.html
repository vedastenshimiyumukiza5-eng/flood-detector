About the Company

Mission

To provide reliable, accessible flood early-warning systems that protect lives and livelihoods, especially in low-connectivity regions.

The problem we solve

Many vulnerable communities lack timely warnings due to poor connectivity and limited infrastructure. Our combined sensor + USSD approach closes that gap.

Vision

To be the trusted partner for governments and communities in building climate-resilient early-warning systems around the world.
